About Reputation Karma

Founded in 2020 by a team of digital marketing veterans and AI specialists, Reputation Karma emerged from a simple observation: businesses were struggling to manage their online reputations in an increasingly digital world. Our mission began with developing smart automation tools to help local businesses collect and manage reviews, and has since evolved into a comprehensive reputation management platform trusted by thousands of businesses worldwide.

Today, we're driven by our core values of transparency, innovation, and customer success. We believe every business deserves the tools to build and maintain a stellar online reputation. Our commitment to continuous innovation and exceptional customer service has made us the preferred choice for businesses seeking to transform their online presence and accelerate growth through authentic customer feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect to see results?

Most clients see significant improvements within 30 days, with review volume typically increasing by 200-300% within the first 90 days. Long-term reputation improvements continue to build over 6-12 months.

Is the review generation process compliant with platform policies?

Yes, our system fully complies with all major platform guidelines including Google, Yelp, and Facebook. We focus on encouraging authentic reviews through automated follow-ups and never engage in fake review generation.

How does the AI-powered response system work?

Our AI analyzes review content and sentiment to generate customized response templates. These maintain your brand voice while addressing specific points in each review, saving time while ensuring personalized engagement.

What platforms do you monitor?

We monitor 50+ platforms including Google, Yelp, Facebook, industry-specific sites, and social media. Custom platform monitoring can be added based on your business needs.

Do you offer custom reporting and analytics?

Yes, our enterprise-grade dashboard can be customized to track your specific KPIs, competitor benchmarking, and ROI metrics. Weekly or monthly reports can be automated and delivered to key stakeholders.
